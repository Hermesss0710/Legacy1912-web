About us

Always Local Always Fresh

Rooted in Heritage, Led with Heart

Legacy 1912 is proudly a female-owned, family-driven establishment led by our Head Chef and owner, Mrs. Jessica Pledger. As both a passionate culinary artist and a devoted wife and mother, Chef Jessica brings heart, heritage, and hospitality to every plate she serves. Her dedication is more than just professional, it's deeply personal. Chef Jessica is the great-great-granddaughter of George E. Sebring, the visionary founder of our beloved town. The name Legacy 1912 is a tribute to that rich family history and the year Sebring was founded a celebration of tradition, community, and enduring values passed down through generations. When you dine with us, you're not just enjoying thoughtfully prepared Southern-inspired cuisine you're stepping into a living story. We invite you to share in our legacy, savor the flavors of our heritage, and experience the warmth that only a truly family-rooted restaurant can offer.
